AN OYO HOTEL



This is one of the most exciting projects done by our company. In 2020, we were contracted to build an Oyo hotel at Chinhut in Lucknow. The hotel is built on an area of over 6500 sq feet, has five stories, and includes a basement and parking area. Some other facilities also built in the hotel were a restaurant, a conference room and a banquet hall. we completed this project on 26th July this year
